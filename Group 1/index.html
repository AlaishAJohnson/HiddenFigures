About Us

Welcome to Hidden Figures, a digital tribute to the often uncelebrated heroes of African-American heritage who have made remarkable strides in the Science, Technology, and Engineering fields. Our mission is to illuminate the extraordinary contributions of individuals who are historically underrepresented or overshadowed by their peers.

Within these pages, we delve into the inspiring journeys and groundbreaking achievements of pioneers like Granville Woods, Katherine G. Johnson, and Dorothy Vaughn. Their stories are just a glimpse into the rich tapestry of African-American excellence in STEM, which has significantly shaped the world we live in today.

In a world where history often overlooks these luminaries, Hidden Figures aims to bring their stories to the forefront. Join us on this captivating exploration of the unsung heroes who defied the odds, shattered barriers, and left an indelible mark on science and technology.

As we uncover the hidden narratives of greatness, we invite you to celebrate their enduring legacy and be inspired by their incredible feats. Together, let's honor these remarkable individuals and their invaluable contributions to the world of STEM.
